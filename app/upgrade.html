May 15th, 2022
Bitcoin Cash network upgrade

The upgrade will enable a variety of new financial products and services by:
  • Supporting arithmetic operations on significantly larger numbers. An expanded arithmetic range will allow contracts to manage very large balances with full precision, enabling contract-based treasuries and improving the efficiency of existing contract designs.
  • Adding new operations, including native introspection operations. These new operations will enable the development of far more secure wallets, efficient recurring payments, and more.
For node operators, upgrade to the latest version of a compatible node:
  • Compatible, released: Bitcoin Cash Node, Bitcoin Verde
  • Yet to release a compatible version: Bitcoin Unlimited, BCHD, Knuth, Flowee the Hub
